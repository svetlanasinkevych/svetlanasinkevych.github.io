what i do?

Hi, I'm a freelance front‑end developer with 3+ years of professional experience.

For the last three years i've been developing responsive front-end interfaces for online-commerce, e-zines, blogs and corporate clients from around the world.

I create responsive crossbrowser layouts that allow users to experience your website in the best and most appropriate way, no matter what kind of browser or device they are using. The use of semantically-correct markup allows a project to be easily maintained and scaled in the future. My main specialization is front-end development, thus my key skills HTML, CSS and javascipt. I offer PSD to HTML layout, design integrations as well as turnkey projects.

Have extensive experience with template engines like Smarty or Twig, hence I develop themes for popular CMS and e-commerce platforms such as: Wordpress, Prestashop and Shoporama. I've successfully implemented design for more then 50 projects and created about 10 projects from scratch.
